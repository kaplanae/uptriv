Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

UpTriv ("we", "us", "our") is a free daily trivia game available at www.uptriv.com. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@uptriv.com.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google

We receive your Google account name, email address, and profile picture. We use this to create your account and display your username on leaderboards and your profile.

Game data

We store your answers, scores, and streaks so you can track your progress over time.

Usage data

We collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, time on site) to understand how people use UpTriv.

How We Use Your Information

  • To run your UpTriv account and save your game history
  • To display leaderboards and friend comparisons
  • To improve the game and fix bugs
  • To show relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

Advertising

UpTriv uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's ad settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, see How Google uses data from sites that use its services.

Cookies

We use cookies to keep you signed in and to remember your game preferences. Google AdSense also uses cookies for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of UpTriv may not work correctly without them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Google — for authentication (Sign in with Google) and advertising (AdSense)
  • Service providers — hosting infrastructure needed to run the site

Data Retention

We keep your account and game history for as long as your account is active. If you'd like your data deleted, email us at hello@uptriv.com and we'll remove it within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

UpTriv is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
